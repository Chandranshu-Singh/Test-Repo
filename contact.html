Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I become a skill provider?

To become a skill provider, simply sign up for an account and complete your profile. You'll need to provide information about your skills, experience, and availability. Our team will review your application and verify your credentials.

What happens if I need to cancel a session?

You can cancel or reschedule sessions up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. Cancellations made within 24 hours may be subject to our cancellation policy, which varies by provider.

How do you ensure quality of skill providers?

We have a rigorous verification process that includes background checks, skill assessments, and reference verification. All providers are also rated by students, helping maintain high quality standards.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your session, contact us within 7 days and we'll work with you to resolve the issue or provide a refund.
