How It All Started

SkillShare was born from a simple observation: while the internet has made information more accessible than ever, finding the right person to learn from remains a challenge. Our founder, Sarah, experienced this firsthand when trying to learn advanced JavaScript concepts.

After struggling with online tutorials and documentation, she connected with a local developer who helped her understand complex concepts in just a few sessions. That experience sparked the idea for SkillShare - a platform that would make it easy for anyone to find and connect with skilled professionals.

What started as a small community of developers in San Francisco has grown into a global platform connecting learners and teachers across hundreds of skill categories. Today, SkillShare serves learners in over 50 countries, helping them achieve their goals through personalized, human-centered learning experiences.
